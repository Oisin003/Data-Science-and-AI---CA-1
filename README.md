# Data-Science-and-AI---CA-1
AI Medical Doctor

Data Science and AI Module – Continuous Assessment 2026 (100%)
BSc (Hons) Applied Computing Yr4 & BSc (Hons) Computing Yr4

Choose one of the following topics:
•	AI Medical Doctor
•	AI Teacher/Educator
•	AI Mental Health Counsellor 
•	AI Barrister/Lawyer 
•	AI Coach/Team Manager for Sport

Based on your chosen AI application, answer parts A and B below. Note, you must use the same application domain for both parts.

Part A – Artificial Intelligence Presentation (50%)
In relation to your chosen domain:
i.	What issues for potential bias/ethical questions exist with your chosen AI? (20%)
ii.	Outline a governance strategy for your chosen AI. (15%)
iii.	What Data Protection/Privacy/Legal issues exist for your chosen AI, if there are none then justify. (15%)
Your answers will be delivered as a presentation which must be done in Pecha Kucha style.  For this presentation, each presenter is only allowed 12 PowerPoint slides to be shown for 30 seconds each (on a timer). In other words, a presenter has only 30 seconds to discuss each slide before the next slide appears, limiting the total presentation time to 6 minutes.  
You should create four slides each for I, ii and iii above.
To make this even more interesting, each slide can only contain a title and an image. It is the image you will talk about for 30 seconds.  You may make your own images, or you may use Generative AI to create an image.  You will also submit a file that includes the prompts you used to create the image. And submit a reference list separately.
This presentation will take place in class on the Monday of Week 8 (9th March)
The AI you consider should be thought of as being in the form of a humanoid robot which is fully autonomous.
